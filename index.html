About Us
JACRES began as an indie game development studio based in the Philippines.  The name is a combination of the founder's initials and the word, "creates".  Its first game was called, "The Explosive Games", which featured sword fights, bombs, and destruction over the seas.
In 2023, JACRES established its new division/organization: Study Assist and Guide for Academics.  It is a reviewer brand that aims to provide quality documents that include summaries of lessons from teachers' materials and discussions.
Today, JACRES aims to not only provide fun for all, but give a helpful boost to everyone.  We are guided by one motto that has been our driving force since the beginning:
Lead the way, together.
