Providing quality reviewers since 2022
Study Assist and Guide for Academics is a non-profit, education-centered organization of JACRES.  In the beginning, reviewers were named with "SAGA" at the end as a simple joke, which signified how far students have come from the past four quarters of their school year.
It was formally established in 2023, receiving a new meaning and identity.  Reviewers evolved from basic fonts with black color to color-coded sections which highlight different sections of information and the hierarchy of importance of the topics.  Ever since its introduction, SAGA has not only assisted students in achieving high grades on exams, but even helped pupils graduate with honors in their school.
JACRES' Study Assist and Guide for Academics continues to provide free, quality reviewers for all students with the motto, "Excel all the way, together."
